1️⃣ Anchoring Strategy (The First Price They See)
Always show the full retail value first.
Example (On Website)
Phuket Luxe Escape
Retail Value: $1,950
Member Experience Price: $1,290
You are not discounting.
You are curating value.
Why This Works
Anchoring makes $1,290 feel like a gain, not a cost.
Never display: “Now only $1,290”
Instead show: “Total Experience Value: $1,950”
Then: “Your Curated Price: $1,290”
